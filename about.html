About Booksy

We're on a mission to make reading accessible to everyone. Discover our story, values, and the passionate team behind your favorite digital library.

Our Story

The idea behind Booksy was born from a common frustration we all experienced: finding free ebooks that are actually legal to read. We noticed that most legal platforms require payment, while free options often operate in grey areas.

As avid readers ourselves, we struggled to access quality books without breaking the bank or compromising on legality. This challenge inspired us to create a solution‚Äîa platform where everyone can enjoy books freely and legally.

That's how Booksy came to life. We built a bridge between readers who want free access to literature and the legal framework that supports authors and publishers. Today, we're proud to offer a legitimate alternative for book lovers everywhere.

Our Mission

This is why we created Booksy‚Äîto build an online library where readers can borrow and read books completely free of charge. Our goal is to make quality literature accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

But our mission goes beyond just serving readers. We also want to create value for authors by giving their works wider exposure and reaching audiences they might not have connected with otherwise. When readers discover new authors through our platform, everyone benefits.

We believe that knowledge and stories should flow freely. By providing a legal, free borrowing system with time limits, we ensure fair access while respecting the rights of content creators. Together, we're building a community where reading enriches lives and empowers minds.
